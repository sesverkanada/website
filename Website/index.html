Speak Up
Canada

We are an independent and voluntary solidarity community that brings together individuals of Turkish origin living in Canada on the basis of republic, democracy, freedom, and human rights. By uniting local initiatives in different cities of Canada under a common umbrella, we aim to create a collective force and organize this power to contribute to the democratic struggle in Turkey. This structure, which rejects discrimination and is committed to pluralism, participation, and universal values, seeks to build a sustainable civil network that shares knowledge, produces common discourse, and can act quickly in times of crisis.
